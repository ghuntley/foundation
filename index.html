Namespace is a cloud-native development and production platform for teams that ship.

It provides an unified development, testing and production workflow so that you can focus on building great product products using state-of-the-art infrastructure.

It's your own platform in a box, fit for your needs. Just add code.

Why Namespace?​

Namespace offers you state-of-the-art development, testing and production workflows that are usually reserved to large organizations with an heavy investment in building an internal platform. These teams combine multiple products and technology so that feature teams can focus on their individual features, rather than fighting infrastructure. Those same capabilities are now also available to you in the Namespace platform.

Build and Development​

Incremental builds: no need to carefully craft Dockerfiles to enable incremental builds. Namespace provides state of the art per-language builders, which still grant you the ability to customize the contents of your images.

Single parallel scheduler across build, test and deploy: Namespace gets you to the finishing line faster by parallelizing as much work as possible: builds run in parallel, and other actions such as tests or deploy are also executed as soon as their results are ready.

Stateful development (local and remote): Start a development session with ns dev and automatically deploy incremental builds, get logs streamed of your application, and meaningful ports automatically forwarded to your local workstation.

Production-like development environments: Long gone are the days to carefully manage your dependencies to enable reproducing production bugs. Namespace management development environments are production-like, out of the box.

Testing and Releasing​

Simple but powerful end-to-end testing: writing an end-to-end integration tests in Namespace is as simple as attaching a simple client with test requests to an existing server or stack.

Fast zero effort CI: Enable Namespace’s continuous integration for one extra layer of convenience: run all of your tests quickly, perform all your builds and automatically deploy to managed production environments. All while getting detailed structured logs and debugging information, with zero configuration.

Cross-language multi-repo and monorepo ready: Either maintain multiple projects in different repository, or host a single large repository. Work across multiple packages within a language, without having to manually manage workspaces, symlinks, etc.

Batteries-included production​

Batteries included: Tracing, Monitoring, Databases, Object stores. Namespace includes out-of-the-box extensions which bring you observability and other capabilities with zero configuration. Have other needs? No problem, our extensible system allows you to pick what technologies to use in your stack.

Multi-platform builds: Using M1-based development machines? Or deploying to AWS Graviton instances? Namespace got you covered performing multi-platform and cross-platform builds as needed.

Cloud native production: Simple on the outside, powerful on the inside. We build on many CNCF technologies to bring you the state of the production in the industry. You have the Kubernetes-level extensibility if you need it – but otherwise don’t need to manage the underlying infrastructure, and use a simplified application-centric control plane instead.
